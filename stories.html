From Clay to Confidence

Priya Sharma

Rampur Village • 22 years

Growing up in Rampur village, I never imagined that the clay I played with as a child would become my path to financial independence. Like most girls in my village, I was expected to marry young and focus on household duties.

Everything changed when I attended an EmpowerHer workshop on traditional crafts. The mentor, Mrs. Gupta, saw potential in my simple clay figurines and encouraged me to develop my skills. She connected me with online marketplaces and taught me how to price my work.

Today, I run a small pottery business that employs six other women from my village. We've preserved our traditional designs while adapting to modern tastes. Last year, we exported our products to three countries!

EmpowerHer didn't just teach me a skill - it gave me the confidence to believe in my own potential. Now I mentor other young girls in my community, showing them that our rural roots can be our greatest strength.
